About Us

Welcome to Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation, a Non Profit Stablished Since 2023.

Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation is committed to promoting equity, empowering individuals, and protecting the environment through sustainable practices. The organization provides training, resources, and support for marginalized groups, conservation efforts, and digital skills development, with a focus on fostering a just and inclusive society.

Key Points:

  • Equity Green Tech Foundation promotes equity, empowerment, and environmental protection through sustainable practices.
  • The organization provides training and resources for individuals to gain digital and technological skills.
  • They actively engage in conservation efforts to protect the environment and promote sustainable practices.
  • The foundation prioritizes the needs of marginalized groups and advocates for social and economic justice.
  • The organization collaborates with businesses and partners to extend their services for sustainable growth and development.
  • Resilient Equity Green Tech Foundation's mission is centered on creating a more just and inclusive society through empowerment and sustainable practices.
